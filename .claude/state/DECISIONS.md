## Decisions

DECISION: Product concept — AI Contract Review Platform (ClauseGuard)
Context: Evaluated 15+ concepts across 3 parallel research agents. Consensus: AI contract analysis for legal professionals has highest revenue velocity (WTP × market size × build speed × margins).
Alternatives considered: ADA Compliance Scanner (#2), AI Email Optimizer (#3), AI Headshots (#4), Chrome Extensions (#5).
Why ClauseGuard: Highest WTP ($149-349/mo), best margins (65-75%), underserved SMB legal market, defensible via domain expertise, proven demand pattern (PDF.ai at $500K+/mo with same model).
Date: 2026-04-02

DECISION: Pricing — $49/$149/$349 charm pricing with "Professional" as Most Popular
Context: Business decision framework — 3 tiers, charm pricing, anchor highest, "Most Popular" on mid tier, annual 20% discount. Legal professionals expect professional pricing; below $49 signals low quality.
Date: 2026-04-02

DECISION: Tech stack — Single HTML + Tailwind CDN
Context: Fastest to ship, zero dependencies, deployable anywhere. Matches AcePilot pattern for static sites. Framework can be added later when app backend is needed.
Date: 2026-04-02

DECISION: Design direction — Dark hero + white body + indigo accent
Context: Navy/dark conveys trust and premium feel for legal audience. Indigo accent is modern without being frivolous. Inter font is professional and highly readable.
Date: 2026-04-02
